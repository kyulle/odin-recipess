Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a rich, cheesy Italian classic layered with pasta, meat sauce, and creamy béchamel.

It’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook noodles until al dente.
  2. Prepare meat sauce with onion, garlic, and tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
  5. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
